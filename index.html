About



I have been with Apple over 6 years. In that time I have worked in over 5 different roles 4 of which were technical. I have worked in four different locations in 3 different states including Florida, California and currently in Washington, DC. I have taken part in multiple pilot programs and helped with the opening of our San Francisco Union Square location. I have trained and assisted with the on boarding of over 350 Apple Retail employees throughout my journey. Currently as the Lead Genius and Technical Advisor in my store I am responsible for leading a technical team of over 50 employees. I have also been actively engaged in shaping our Genius Bar training through planning and executing the on boarding experience with the participants and sharing feedback with our remote trainers.

Outside of work I am familiar with HTML5, CSS, javaScript, jQuery, Node.js, Express.js, MySQL, MongoDB, JSON, AJAX, API's, Firebase and the command line. I have completed over 100 hours on freecodecamp.org and am currently taking part in a 6 month Full Stack Web Development bootcamp at The George Washington University through February. I feel that my skills with these technologies will apply to multiple web development roles that are available in the market today. In my current role I am considered a strong resource for technical guidance. As I become more knowledgeable in my Web Developer role I can be counted on to help more junior employees as I am enthusiastic about helping people grow and develop. I am extremely passionate about technology and learning. Programming as a career would be a dream come true. Thank you for taking the time to read this bio.
